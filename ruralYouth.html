Empowering youth in rural India with job readiness skills

Program Brief

The youth in rural India have limited exposure to 21st century skills which results in diminishing their prospects of securing desired opportunities.The Rural Youth Upskilling Program aims to improve the employability skills of rural youth aged 18-25 by training them on essential 21st century skills.

Sessions Covered

We covered the following:

  • Soft skills: Self-awareness, growth mindset, critical thinking, decision-making, communication.
  • Job readiness: Resume building, job description analysis.

 


  • Digital literacy: MS Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
  • Entrepreneurial mindset: Cultivating entrepreneurial skills.
